Money Manager

AuraQuest presents it's tool to help manage everyones favorite thing in the world, money. In order for you to have aura you must have money and with this tool we will take your monthly income as your budget and allow you to set your savings goal, along with that we will allow you to input your expenses into categories to help you map out your spending and get into healthy spending habits. Finally we will display your progress towards your savings goal at the bottom with a color coded text based on how much money you inputted into savings (Green=100%+, Yellow=99%-70%, Red=69%-0%).
