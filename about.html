About AuraQuest

AuraQuest is a gamefied platform focused on helping users improve their habits, wellness, and self-awareness.

Healthy Habits Tracker

The Healthy Habit tracker allows users to log their daily habits such as their meals, water consumption, sleep and exercise.

  • Daily habit tracking
  • Wellness metrics updating automatically
  • Feedback using bars, badges and emojis

Personal Journal

The Personal Journal allows users to reflect on their day, express their thoughts and feelings, and track their mood over time.

  1. Daily journal entries
  2. Mood and mindfulness tracking
  3. Review past reflections

Money Manager

The Money Manager helps users track their expenses, set budgets, and manage their finances effectively.

Achievement System

The Achievement System rewards users for completing goals and milestones. Users can earn badges, unlock achievements, and track their progress.
